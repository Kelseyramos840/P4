Balanced

This is my experience as a student athlete.

Mostly every day I wake up around 6:30am to get ready for my 8am practice. I go to practice every morning and on Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday we have lifts. Depending on my schedule for that semester, I have classes in the afternoon which vary. Usually I'm done by the latest 4, this gives me time to do work and hang out with my teammates. Sometimes I'll get an extra workout in for mental relief. Like a nice bike or walk. On the weekend, in season it's hard to do anything crazy but in the spring since it's our off season i'll hang out with my friends and family. We will go to the city pretty often since we are only 30 minutes away. I love the balance this life offers me. You get to wake up early, get your workout in and start the day off with my favorite activity. Then I get to go to classes and have the rest of the day for myself.
