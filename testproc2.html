Tips and Mindset

Coming in as an athlete your first year of college can be tough. Transititioning from high school to college is hard as is. Now add being an athlete into the mix. Here are some tips moving forward.

On the Field
  • If you think your working hard, work even harder
  • Never give up
  • Be a good teammate, dont be selfish, but know when to put yourself first
  • Ask for help
  • blank
  • blank
    • Crucial for Athletes
  • Eat to fuel and hydrate!
  • Prioritize sleep! 8 or more hours
  • Listen to podcasts or read a book from other athletes. This can help you grow into a good mindset
  • Make sure you have a rest day!
  • Get into the trainers to reduce the risk of injuries
  • Stretch before and after your workouts
    • Off the Field
  • Build good relationships with your teammates, coaches, and professors
  • Time management, stay organized!
  • Go the extra mile, work hard!
  • Talk to people that have been there longer for advice
  • If you need help, don't be afraid to ask
  • Take the constructive criticism and grow from it
    • How to be a Leader
  • Learn from not just your mistakes but also others
  • Understand that you are going to make mistakes and own them, accountability is key.
  • Don't be afraid to speak up
  • Have confidence
  • Build relationships so people will trust you
  • Have your actions match your words
  • Do the right thing all the time, not just in front of the coach
  • Off the field leading is just as important as on the field
